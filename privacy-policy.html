Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to UB Institute & Training ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or enroll in our courses.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, address, educational background when you fill out forms or enroll in courses.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, UPI IDs, bank account information for course fee payments (processed securely through Razorpay).
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other diagnostic data.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Processing course enrollments and payments
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries
  • Sending course updates, newsletters, and promotional materials (with your consent)
  • Improving our website and services
  • Placement assistance and connecting students with employers
  • Generating certificates upon course completion

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Placement Partners: For job placement assistance (only with your consent)
  • Payment Processors: Razorpay for secure payment processing
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Cookies Policy

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies separately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
