What courses does UB Institute offer?

UB Institute offers a wide range of courses including:

  • Digital Marketing: Complete DM, SEO, Social Media, Google Ads
  • Computer Courses: Basic Computer, Advanced Computer, MS Office, Web Development, Python
  • Accounting: Tally Prime with GST
  • Vocational: Data Entry, Graphic Design

We also provide Career Guidance and Job Placement assistance.

What is the duration of courses?

Course duration varies based on the program:

  • Digital Marketing: 1-3 months
  • Basic Computer: 3 months
  • Advanced Computer: 6 months
  • Tally with GST: 2 months
  • Web Development: 4 months
  • Graphic Design: 3 months

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, absolutely! We provide 100% placement assistance to all eligible students. Our placement support includes:

  • Professional resume building
  • Interview preparation and mock interviews
  • Soft skills training
  • Direct job referrals to 100+ partner companies
  • LinkedIn profile optimization

Our placement rate is over 90% for eligible candidates.

What are the fee payment options?

We offer flexible payment options for your convenience:

  • One-time Payment: Pay full fee upfront and get 5% discount
  • EMI Option: Pay in 2-3 easy installments (No extra charges)
  • Payment Methods: Cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe), Bank Transfer, Razorpay

Special scholarships available for meritorious and economically weaker students.

Are the courses available online?

Yes! We offer both Online and Offline learning modes:

  • Offline: Classroom training at our institute (Most courses)
  • Online: Live interactive sessions via Zoom/Google Meet (Selected courses)
  • Hybrid: Combination of online theory and offline practical sessions

Contact us to know which mode is available for your preferred course.

What certification will I receive?

Upon successful course completion, you will receive:

  • UB Institute Certificate: Course completion certificate
  • Industry Certificates: Google, HubSpot, Facebook certifications (for eligible courses)
  • Government Recognized: Certificates valid for government jobs (selected courses)

All certificates include a unique verification code for authenticity.

What is the batch timing?

We offer flexible batch timings to suit your schedule:

  • Morning Batch: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Afternoon Batch: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
  • Evening Batch: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
  • Weekend Batch: Saturday & Sunday special batches

Custom timing can be arranged for group admissions (5+ students).

How can I enroll for a course?

Enrolling is easy! You can join us through any of these methods:

  1. Visit Our Institute: Come to our center for counseling and direct admission
  2. Call Us: +91 6205 119 312
  3. WhatsApp: Send a message to +91 6205 119 312
  4. Online Form: Fill the enquiry form on this website
  5. Email: Send your query to UBCENTERS@GMAIL.COM

Our counselor will guide you through the admission process.
